Hi, I'm Rogelyn Bagnol Estano.

I have a passion for exploring and experiencing different cuisines, and I'm excited to share my love for food with you!

The Philippines is known for its rich culinary heritage and diverse flavors. From savory adobo to refreshing halo-halo, Filipino cuisine offers a unique blend of influences from various cultures, including Spanish, Chinese, Malay, and American.

What makes Filipino food truly special is not just the taste but also the stories and traditions behind each dish. Whether it's the comfort of a warm bowl of sinigang or the festive spirit of a boodle fight, Filipino food brings people together and creates unforgettable memories.

Join me on a culinary journey through the Philippines as we explore the best food this beautiful country has to offer!
